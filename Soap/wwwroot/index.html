Bookmarklet (recommended for reposts) drag the button to your bookmarks bar

yt-dlp can't enumerate the reposts tab. Workaround: a tiny script that runs in your browser tab, scrapes video links from the page, and sends them here.

  1. Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible (Chrome/Firefox: Ctrl+Shift+B).
  2. Drag this button onto it: 📥 Soap reposts
  3. Go to your TikTok reposts page: tiktok.com/@yourname → click the Reposts tab.
  4. Click the Soap reposts bookmark. It scrolls to the bottom, collects every video link, and POSTs them here.
  5. Switch back to this tab — the gallery fills up as videos finish downloading.

Tip: scroll the reposts page a bit yourself first so TikTok knows to load thumbnails. The bookmarklet auto-scrolls until no new links appear for ~3.5s.

TikTok cookies …

The reposts tab needs a logged-in session. Paste your TikTok cookie header — no extensions, no extra tools.

  1. Log into tiktok.com in your browser.
  2. Press F12 → Network tab → reload the page if the list is empty.
  3. Click any request to tiktok.com → scroll the right panel to Request Headers.
  4. Copy the line that starts with Cookie: (or just the value after it).
  5. Paste it below and click Save.
